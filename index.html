About Re-Entry Navigation Service

If you or someone you know is returning to New York City after release from the New York State prison system (NYS Department of Corrections and Community Supervision, or DOCCS), assistance in establishing yourself for a crime-free and successful re-entry is necessary. Priority is given to individuals referred to RNS from the The Parole Preparation Project.
