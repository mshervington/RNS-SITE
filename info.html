Real-world life is not like a hip-hop video. Upon release, a returning citizen should focus their time and energy on:

    Health Care Stable Housing Employment Handling Money Release Conditions

    1. Health Care

      Incarcerated citizens receive sub-standard medical care. Sometimes they are released with unknown medical issues that could prove fatal if not properly diagnosed and treated.

    2. Stable Housing

      A safe, clean and stable place to live after release is one of the most critical needs for a returning citizen.

    3. Employment

      Being able to provide for one's own needs empowers a returning citizen to become and remain self-sufficient. Finding and maintaining steady legal employment is a must.

    4. Handling Money

      "Gate money" won't last forever in the real world. To maintain yourself you must be wise about handling your money.

    5. Release Conditions

      To remain free and able to function on your own, following the conditions of your parole release is most important.
